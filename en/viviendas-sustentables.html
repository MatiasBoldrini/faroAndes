Refugios del futuro

Throughout history, the designs of homes used by communities have evolved to meet their needs, utilizing available materials and different technologies. With the advent of climate change, there is a growing focus on innovative designs that optimize the use of energy and water while incorporating environmentally friendly materials.

The goal of this project is to design versatile, small-sized homes that make sustainable use of resources and can be easily transported. The key areas of focus will be:

  • Innovative materials and designs: Incorporating locally generated materials from waste, recycled, and low-impact materials promotes a circular economy and efficient resource use. The design will consider energy efficiency and space optimization.
  • Energy generation from renewable sources: Providing energy for the homes from renewable sources, so that the potentially mobile housing modules are virtually self-sufficient.
  • Off-grid water supply and reuse system: Water supply is proposed to be sourced from the vapor present in the air, and effluents will be treated so that wastewater can be used for irrigation or other purposes within the house. Additionally, systems for collecting and storing rainwater are proposed.
  • Smart resource management system: Continuous connectivity service with an automated resource management system using artificial intelligence. This allows for the incorporation of high-complexity devices without burdening the user.
