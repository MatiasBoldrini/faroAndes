Aerial Systems Propulsion with hydrogen

Global economic growth, increasing population, and technological advancements are leading to a rise in the global demand for primary energy. Given that most of this energy is currently supplied by fossil fuels, a significant amount of greenhouse gases is emitted, contributing to climate change. As a result, most countries are adopting policies focused on reducing carbon emissions.

Hydrogen is a clean and renewable energy source. The development of hydrogen energy is a common goal pursued by many countries to combat the current trend of global warming.

Within this social, economic, cultural, and natural framework, the construction of a hydrogen-powered aircraft prototype is proposed. The plan is to design and build an airplane and an electric motor glider that will fly using liquid hydrogen, a CO2-free alternative that would greatly support aviation and contribute to sustainable development.
